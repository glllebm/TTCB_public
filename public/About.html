About club TTCB

Hi! I’m Gleb, the founder of the TTCB club and rating system.

Our community brings together table tennis players of all skill levels, including complete beginners, to have a good time and improve their game.

Every week we organize tournaments in different formats on weekends, and once a week we also get together to play and practice.

Subscribe to our channel to stay up to date with our tournaments and practice sessions.

TTCB Rating

We have our own rating system, created to help every player track their progress. For those who enjoy competing at their best, it also provides an extra challenge and a clear goal to climb higher in the rankings.
